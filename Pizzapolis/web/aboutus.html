PIZZAPOLIS

About us

Since opening its' doors 35 years ago Pizzapolis has served only the same award-winning delicious pizza, using the best quality Italian, California and local ingredients, including the freshest hand-cut vegetables.

Pizzapolis's tomato sauce is the highest quality. Made from only grade-A tomatoes picked and packed fresh in season. Only the best tasting cheese is used on every pizza served. Pizzapolis prides itself on this rich tradition of mouth-water pizza and superior service. At Pizzapolis pleasing the customer comes first. Pizzapolis is motivated by the quality and taste of the product it serves.

Come to Pizzapolis and we'll make you the freshest, best tasting, highest quality pizza you'll ever eat. We promise once you eat our pizza, you'll never go anywhere else. Pizzapolis, an international award winning pizza treasure, right here in your neighborhood.

Our People

Our people are the difference makers at Pizzapolis. We are a family owned company that values honesty, teamwork, punctuality, dedication and loyalty. We stress consistency in quality, service, and food offered in a unique environment by a staff that is committed to one another, our customer, and our community.
